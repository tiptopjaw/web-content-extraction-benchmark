Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) Can I switch platforms later if I start with one and outgrow it? Yes, but it can be time-consuming. Some platforms let you export course content, while others don’t. Always check migration support before committing. What’s the difference between a course platform and a course marketplace? Platforms like Thinkific or Kajabi let you control pricing, branding, and student relationships. Marketplaces like Udemy give you access to a large audience but take a cut and limit customization. Do I need a separate website if I use one of these platforms? Most modern platforms (like Kajabi, Thinkific, and Podia) come with built-in website builders. But if you already have a website, you can usually link or embed your course pages. Which platforms support mobile learning? Thinkific, Kajabi, Teachable, Circle, and Mighty Networks all offer mobile-friendly access. Circle and Kajabi even offer branded mobile apps (on higher plans). What happens to my content if I cancel my plan or leave a platform? Your content typically becomes inaccessible to students, and some platforms may delete it after a grace period. Always back up your materials. Can I sell other digital products like ebooks, templates, or coaching sessions? Yes. Platforms like Kajabi, Thinkific, Teachable, Podia, and Circle allow you to sell a mix of courses, downloads, coaching, and even memberships. Which platform is best if I already use ConvertKit, Mailchimp, or another email tool? Teachable and LearnDash are great if you want to plug into external email tools. Kajabi, Thinkific, and Podia also integrate easily but come with built-in email marketing. How important is SCORM compliance for online courses? SCORM is mainly useful for corporate or academic settings. If you’re selling to individuals, you likely don’t need it. LearnWorlds and LearnDash are SCORM-friendly if you do. What payment gateways do these platforms support? Most use Stripe and PayPal. Some also support Buy Now, Pay Later options like Klarna or Afterpay (Thinkific recently added these). Can I offer coupons, discounts, or free trials on these platforms? Yes. Almost all paid plans across platforms like Teachable, Kajabi, Podia, and Thinkific let you create promo codes, bundles, and free trials.
