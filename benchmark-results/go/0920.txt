Taking care of your teeth is essential to maintaining a healthy smile. While regular visits to the dentist are a must, the right dental products can make a significant difference in your daily oral care routine. Poor dental health can also have a negative impact on your mental health, leading to increased stress, anxiety, and self-esteem issues. Here are 7 top products that Barton Oak dentists recommend to help you achieve and maintain optimal oral health: Electric Toothbrush – Philips Sonicare or Oral-B Power Brush Electric toothbrushes are often recommended by dentists for their superior ability to remove plaque compared to manual brushing. Both the Philips Sonicare and Oral-B Power Brush are great options, offering advanced brushing technology that can improve your gum health and provide a deeper clean. Electric Flosser – ProSys JetFloss or Waterpik Flossing is essential to remove food particles and plaque that get stuck between your teeth, but many people struggle with traditional flossing. Electric flossers, such as the ProSys JetFloss and Waterpik, make this task much easier and more effective. These devices use water pressure to flush away debris between your teeth and along the gumline, which is especially helpful for those with braces, implants, or other dental work. Fluoride Toothpaste – Fluoridex or Prevident 5000 Fluoride is a powerful tool in the fight against cavities, and Fluoridex or Prevident 5000 are fluoride toothpastes highly recommended by dental professionals. It helps remineralize tooth enamel, making it more resistant to decay. Regular use of fluoride toothpaste can help prevent tooth sensitivity, reduce plaque buildup, and provide an extra layer of protection against cavities. Fluoridex and Prevident 5000 are both prescription only, call us today if you're interested in a prescription strength toothpaste. Floss – Glide or Superfloss For those who prefer traditional flossing, Glide and Superfloss are two of the best options available. Glide dental floss is designed to easily slide between teeth, even those with tight spaces, without shredding. Superfloss, on the other hand, features a three-in-one design that includes a stiffened end for easy insertion, a spongy portion for cleaning around appliances like braces, and a regular floss section for cleaning between teeth. VivaStyle Whitening Strips For those looking for a safe and effective way to achieve a whiter smile at home, VivaStyle is a great option. These strips work to remove surface stains and lighten the color of your teeth without causing irritation. Regular cleaning of your dental devices can help prevent bad odors and bacteria buildup, contributing to your overall oral health. Alcohol-Free Mouth Rinse – StellaLife or ACT Mouth rinses are essential for freshening your breath and providing extra protection against plaque and gingivitis. Dentists often recommend alcohol-free options like StellaLife and ACT because they are less harsh on the mouth and gums.
