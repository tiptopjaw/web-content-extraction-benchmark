Signs You Need Electrical Repair Electrical systems are comprised of an intricate network of wiring, switches, and outlets. Issues are often tied to a faulty breaker panel. As the hub of your home’s electrical wiring, the breaker panel is responsible for distributing electricity through multiple circuits to power lights, appliances, and devices plugged into the wall. Watch for these signs that your electrical panel is failing or your wiring needs repair: Frequently tripping circuit breakers Flickering, dimming, or buzzing lights The need for excessive extension cords Sparks or arcing when plugging in appliances Electric shock upon unplugging devices Discolored, loose, or warm outlets A burning odor coming from your outlets A malfunctioning electrical system could be dangerous.
