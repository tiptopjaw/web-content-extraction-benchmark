A keyword research tool helps you figure out which topics are actually worth creating content for based on what people are already searching for. These tools give you a ton of data, but here are the only three metrics I really care about: Monthly Search Volume: How many people search for a keyword like “how to use SEO on Instagram” every month? If it’s under 100? Not really worth creating content around. Ranking Difficulty Score: This tells me if a keyword is too to rank for. My rule of thumb? Anything over that is too competitive, which means you’ll never rank. difficult Intent: Is the keyword informational? Then it needs to be a blog or TikTok video. Is it commercial or transactional?
