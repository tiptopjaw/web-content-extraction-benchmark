Personal Training Maximize your workouts with the help of a certified personal trainer who will design a custom plan to help you achieve your fitness goals. Personal training sessions are available for UT students and RecSports members. General Information All personal trainers maintain a nationally recognized personal training certification. Sessions can take place at any of the seven RecSports facilities. Currently enrolled UT students and RecSports members are eligible to purchase personal training sessions. Training Packages You may choose to purchase a package to train one-on-one or with a buddy. All sessions are 60 minutes in length. All packages include a complimentary initial 30-minute consultation. Sessions expire 365 days from the date of purchase. You may only purchase the Get Started with Personal Training package once. One-on-One Training Fee Get Started with Personal Training Designed for participants who are new to personal training.
