Rental Cars The Rental Car Center is located across from Terminal B at 1639 Airport Blvd. – Terminal B passengers can walk across Airport Boulevard at the crosswalks from baggage claim to reach the Rental Car Center. Terminal A and International Arrival passengers may take any Airport shuttle bus to or from the Rental Car Center. Hours vary; please contact your rental company for after-hours service. To return a vehicle, follow the signs to the Rental Car Center and drive up the designated ramp.
