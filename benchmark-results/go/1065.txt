Creating wedding menus that reflect every couple’s story, Ferah Catering brings flavor and ease to celebrations across DFW. When couples start planning their wedding, one of the biggest decisions they face is choosing the right caterer. Food is at the heart of celebration, it’s how cultures come together, how families connect, and how guests remember the day. But with so many options, cuisines, and costs to consider, where do you start? At Ferah Catering, weddings are more than just meals. They’re customized culinary experiences. With a team of chefs from around the world, Ferah specializes in creating menus that reflect each couple’s unique story, honoring multicultural traditions, adding a little Texas flair, or designing a chef action station that delights guests. Tip #1: Personalize Your Menu Couples often assume they’ll have to choose from preset catering packages, but Ferah takes a different approach. “Our goal is to listen first,” says Co-Founder, Stephanie Özcan. “We want to know your vision, your favorite flavors, even your family traditions. Then we build a menu that reflects that.” Texas-shaped chocolate cakes, locally sourced bourbon cocktails, and a risotto bar with chefs cooking in front of guests showcase Ferah’s customizable catering style, ensuring every guest finds something memorable on the plate. Tip #2: Don’t Be Afraid To Mix Cuisines Couples often feel pressure to stick to a single type of cuisine, but blending flavors from different cultures can make your wedding menu truly memorable. Ferah encourages couples to explore combinations that reflect their personal tastes and backgrounds. You might pair Mediterranean small plates with Southern comfort favorites, or add a globally inspired dessert station to surprise guests. Ferah’s blog offers curated menu suggestions from their in-house chefs, making it easy to explore different flavor combinations and see how diverse dishes can work together. Using these sample menus as inspiration allows couples to create a menu that tells their unique story, without feeling overwhelmed. Tip #3: Trends Can Be Your Friend Wedding catering food trends are constantly evolving, and Ferah is seeing two clear favorites this year. Elevated mocktails are a crowd favorite, and signature alcohol-free drinks are quickly becoming a wedding staple. Ferah creates craft mocktails that can stand alone or be customized with a splash of spirits, making them perfect for celebrations. Interactive culinary experiences are also on the rise. Couples are embracing dining as entertainment, with options like carving stations and the “drunken charcuterie,” a spread of cheese infused with liquor and spices. Tip #4: Look Beyond the Price Tag Budget is always top of mind, but Ferah emphasizes that couples should look deeper than cost alone. “Responsiveness, professionalism, and trust are just as important,” they share. Ferah’s transparency of listing prices directly on their website, sets them apart in an industry where costs are often hidden until late in the process. They also encourage couples to ask key questions such as whether the caterer is fully insured, if they have certified bartenders, and what past clients say about them. Ferah’s reviews highlight both their exceptional service and their ability to deliver exactly what they promise. Tip #5: Don’t Overlook the Details Many couples forget about vendor meals or miss fine print in venue contracts. Photographers, planners, and other vendors may require meals, and Ferah includes those options in proposals so there are no last-minute surprises. Venue contracts can also limit catering options. Some require couples to choose from a short list of providers. Ferah partners with dozens of DFW venues but also caters anywhere with an open vendor policy. Reading the fine print early prevents unexpected costs. Tip #6: Seek Service Beyond the Plate What sets Ferah apart isn’t just their globally inspired food, it’s their service model. Every couple works with both a Catering Concierge, who helps design the menu, and a Catering Coordinator, who manages venue details and logistics. “We don’t just show up on your wedding day,” Stephanie Özcan says. “We walk alongside you from the moment you book until the last guest leaves.” Final Tips from the Ferah Team Couples looking for the right caterer should read reviews, ask the right questions, and pay attention to how responsive and professional a team feels. With a commitment to transparency, customization, and hospitality, they’re helping couples across DFW bring that vision to life.
