LinkedIn is generally the most expensive platform, with an average Cost-Per-Click (CPC) often exceeding $5.00. The high cost is due to its valuable audience of professionals, making it a powerful, albeit pricey, tool for B2B marketing and recruitment. CPC (Cost-Per-Click) means you pay every time someone clicks on your ad. This model is best for campaigns where the goal is to drive traffic, generate leads, or make sales. CPM (Cost-Per-Mille) means you pay for every 1,000 times your ad is shown (impressions). This model is better for campaigns focused on building brand awareness and maximizing visibility.
