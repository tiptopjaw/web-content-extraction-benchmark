Welcome to the Newsroom The American Dental Hygienists’ Association (ADHA ® ) is the largest and oldest organization representing the professional interests of the more than 220,000 dental hygienists in the United States. Dental hygienists are preventive oral health professionals, licensed in dental hygiene, who provide educational, clinical and therapeutic services that support total health through the promotion of optimal oral health.
