Small Business Enterprise Certification Programs Certified firms may qualify under one or more of the following categories: Small Business Enterprise – Architectural & Engineering (SBE-A&E) Small Business Enterprise – Construction (SBE-CON) Small Business Enterprise – Goods and Services (SBE-GS) Local Developing Business (LDB) Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) Airport Concessionaire Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (ACDBE) Apply for SBE Certification Programs online through the Business Management Workforce System. For questions or assistance, contact the SPD Certification Unit at 305-375-3111 or [email protected] .
