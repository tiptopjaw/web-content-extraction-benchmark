ML consulting FAQ What engagement models do you offer? For machine learning projects, we offer the following engagement models: Full project outsourcing We take over your ML project and implement it end-to-end while enabling you to monitor our progress. Dedicated team We assemble and manage a team of selected ML experts to work alongside your in-house or outsourced specialists on your ML project. Staff augmentation We provide one or more ML specialists who will join your team and work under your management. How do you integrate the ML solution with our legacy systems? At the discovery stage, we analyze your existing technology environment and, drawing on this information, conceptualize the ML solution and define its components. If you want a fully-integrated solution, we can perform the necessary integrations; otherwise, we will pass all the necessary information and documentation to your in-house team. Do we need the infrastructure to train ML models? No, you don’t. Itransition can train custom machine learning models on local servers with increased CPU power as part of our service offering.
