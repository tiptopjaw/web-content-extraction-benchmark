Correlation vs. Causation • Distinguishing real ranking factors from seasonal or competitive changes demands rigorous testing. Volume of Data • Sifting through thousands of keywords, pages, and behavioral metrics can be overwhelming. Technical Debt • Legacy code, slow servers, or outdated CMSs can mask content improvements. Continuous Evolution • Algorithms now update in near real-time, so yesterday’s solution may need tweaking today. Pitfall: Over-Optimizing for One Signal Remedy: Balance content quality, technical health, and backlinks—Google’s algorithm is multi-dimensional.
