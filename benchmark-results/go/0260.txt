New Law is a Win for Education & Certification Programs Posted July 9, 2025 We are happy to share that elevating your education and certifications will soon get a lot easier! The Freedom to Invest in Tomorrow’s Workforce Act was signed into law this past weekend and has been a key issue on our policy agenda that we have advocated for with others in the association space. This legislation expands the eligible uses of the tax-favorable 529 savings plan to cover costs associated with workforce training and credentialing programs. Thanks to all of you who helped lend a supporting voice on this issue!
