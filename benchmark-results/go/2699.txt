Lunacal is an AI-powered online appointment booking and scheduling system designed to streamline the scheduling process for businesses of all sizes. By integrating with multiple calendar platforms and offering customizable features, Lunacal aims to enhance efficiency and professionalism in managing appointments. Key Features Calendar Integration : Lunacal seamlessly syncs with Google, Outlook, and Apple calendars, allowing users to manage multiple calendars simultaneously. Customizable Booking Pages : Users can personalize their booking pages with various themes, colors, and content, including images, videos, and social media feeds, to align with their brand identity. Automated Reminders and Workflows : The system sends automated email and SMS reminders for meetings and can trigger multi-step emails before, during, and after appointments to enhance communication. Team Scheduling : Lunacal offers features like round-robin and collective scheduling, enabling teams to distribute meetings evenly and coordinate appointments when all members are available. Custom Availability and Buffers : Users can set specific availability for different days, add buffers between appointments, and define minimum notice periods for bookings to manage their schedules effectively. Multi-Location and Multi-Service Management : The platform supports managing multiple business locations and service categories, facilitating comprehensive scheduling solutions. Custom Domains and Branding : Lunacal allows users to use their own domain for a fully branded scheduling experience and offers options to remove Lunacal branding from booking pages. Integrations : The system integrates with video conferencing tools (e.g., Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Zoom), payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, PayPal), and CRM platforms (e.g., HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce) to streamline operations. Who is it for? Lunacal is suitable for a wide range of users, including: Professionals : Individuals seeking a reliable and customizable scheduling solution. Growing Businesses : Companies needing team scheduling capabilities and advanced features to manage appointments efficiently. Large Enterprises : Organizations requiring personalized onboarding, training, and dedicated support to handle complex scheduling needs. Pricing Lunacal offers three pricing tiers to cater to different user requirements: Standard : $9 per user per month, suitable for professionals, includes unlimited events, PayPal and Zapier integration, and priority support. Teams : $15 per user per month, designed for growing businesses, includes all standard features plus team scheduling pages, round-robin scheduling, and collective scheduling. Enterprise : $25 per user per month, tailored for large companies, includes all team features plus an account manager, custom integrations, phone support, and personalized onboarding and training. Final Thoughts Lunacal provides a comprehensive suite of features aimed at simplifying appointment scheduling and enhancing team coordination. Its customizable booking pages, automated workflows, and robust integrations make it a versatile tool for businesses looking to improve their scheduling processes.
