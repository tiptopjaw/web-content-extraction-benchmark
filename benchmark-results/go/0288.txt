Certification and Training Programs Learn to inspire the Pack with one of our certification and training programs. Participants will gain hands-on experience in a supportive and inclusive environment led by our team of fitness and wellness experts. FitWell Award Wellness and Recreation is committed to creating high-impact opportunities for all students. The FitWell Award is application-based with a holistic review of each student’s experience and interest in the course(s). This award only applies to courses offered by Wellness and Recreation during any given semester and may include the following courses: Fitness Instructor Training Program, ACE Health Coach and NASM Personal Trainer. Please see course-specific information above. Award applications are available for submission each academic year and are meant to further the mission and philosophy of Wellness and Recreation. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis as space is available. You will be contacted directly via email if you are selected. Partners Wellness and Recreation is able to offer these nationally-recognized fitness and health workshops and certification courses through our partnerships with the American Council on Exercise ® , the National Academy of Sports Medicine ™ and LesMills International ™ .
