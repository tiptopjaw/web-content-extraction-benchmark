Preparing Your Space for a Cleaning Service Preparing Your Space for a Cleaning Service 1. Declutter Before the Cleaners Arrive Cleaning teams focus on deep cleaning , not organizing clutter. Decluttering helps them work efficiently. Quick Decluttering Checklist: Keep: Essential items you use daily. Store: Items you need but don’t use frequently. Discard: Unused or unnecessary clutter. Tip: Use labeled storage bins to maintain order after the cleaning service is complete. 2. Identify & Communicate Your Cleaning Priorities Understanding your cleaning needs helps ensure satisfaction. List specific areas that need special attention (e.g., stains, dust buildup). Inform cleaners about off-limits zones or delicate items. Schedule a pre-cleaning walkthrough if necessary. Follow a cleaning service checklist to organize tasks effectively. 3. Ensure Pet & Child Safety Cleaning products and equipment may not be safe for pets or small children. Keep pets in a separate room or arrange for them to be outside. Inform cleaners about any pet-related allergies or sensitivities. If possible, schedule cleaning during school or daycare hours. 4. Secure Valuables & Fragile Items Move delicate items like glass decor or electronics to a safe place. Notify cleaners about fragile possessions that require extra care. Label any off-limits cabinets or drawers. Ensure professional house cleaning preparation by securing sensitive materials. 5. Provide Access & Clear Instructions Ensure doors and gates are unlocked or provide necessary access codes. Leave a list of priorities or focus areas if you won’t be present. Confirm equipment availability if the service provider doesn’t bring their own supplies. Here is a Bonus For You! Fill This Survey and Get Great Bonuses and Perks!
