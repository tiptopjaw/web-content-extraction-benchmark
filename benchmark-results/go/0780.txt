A quick online search will reveal a multitude of options. To save you time, we’ve compiled a list of the top lawyer review sites to elevate your online presence. Avvo Founded in Seattle, Avvo provides comprehensive information to help people make informed decisions when choosing a lawyer. With more than eight million monthly visitors to its directory, it stands out as one of the premier review sites for lawyers. Avvo boasts an extensive database covering nearly every licensed lawyer in the US, with an impressive 97% of lawyers rated on it. Lawyers can claim their profile on the platform free of charge. Along with client reviews, each profile includes an Avvo rating, which is based on a scale of 1 (Extreme Caution) to 10 (Superb). Attaining a perfect 10 is a major accomplishment, one that many lawyers proudly display on marketing channels like their websites. Avvo ratings are calculated based on information from lawyer profiles, state bar associations, and other organizations that license legal professionals. The following information will be considered when calculating it, so be sure to include as many details as you can on: Work experience Awards Speaking engagements Publications Peer endorsements Avvo offers free basic profiles with Avvo Starter, while enhanced profiles with Avvo Base and Martindale-Avvo Complete Profile Plus come at an additional fee. Lawyers.com Lawyers.com stands out as another prominent player among lawyer review sites, with over 100,000 daily active users. This free service is offered by Martindale-Hubbell, a trusted publisher of lawyer and law firm directories since 1868. With its user-friendly search tool, visitors can effortlessly find attorneys based on their location and across more than 150 practice areas. One of Lawyers.com’s standout features is its ability to provide instant access to reviews by former clients and legal professionals. Much like Avvo, the directory features 95% of all practicing attorneys in the US—equivalent to about one million lawyers. When potential clients are ready to contact a lawyer, they can easily do so directly through the site. LegalMatch Established in 1999, LegalMatch is a free, web-based exchange designed to assist individuals and small businesses in making informed decisions when selecting a lawyer. The platform has gained a substantial following, drawing around one million monthly visitors seeking a simple method for finding legal representation. After answering a brief set of questions, users are matched with pre-screened lawyers in their city who specialize in the relevant legal practice area. Lawyer profiles feature reviews from past clients, using a five-star rating system that evaluates factors like response time, legal knowledge, and price—as well as detailed commentary on their experiences. Once a lawyer receives an email about the potential case, they can respond to the ones they’re interested in. Attorneys can gain free access to the platform with a seven-day guest pass. Membership fees, which start at $455 per month, are based on location and legal specialization. Justia The Justia Lawyer Directory is a free online resource offering detailed profiles of lawyers, law firms, and non-profit legal service and legal aid organizations across the US. With over 12 million visitors per month, it ranks among the top-visited legal websites in the US. Every lawyer or organization can create a comprehensive, no-cost profile on the directory, showcasing their practice areas, experience, education, fees, full contact information, and more. They can also include links to online presences, such as blogs and social media profiles. Justia offers two types of reviews on its site. The Justia Lawyer Rating system provides an attorney-to-attorney review, using a scale of 1 to 10 to compare lawyers based on factors like legal knowledge and communication skills. In addition to this, the Justia Lawyer Directory offers a Client Reviews service that allows clients to share their insights into lawyers’ services and help others decide if a lawyer is the right fit for their needs. While not strictly a lawyer review site, Google is often one of the first places many clients go when searching for a lawyer. Law firms can receive Google reviews by creating a free Business Profile and encouraging clients to provide feedback on their experiences. To create a Google Business Profile, you’ll need to include essential details about your businesses, including contact information and website. How to get more lawyer reviews Enhancing your online presence with positive attorney reviews is key to establishing trust and attracting new clients. To garner more glowing reviews on attorney review sites, consider the following tips: Deliver outstanding service. From the initial consultation to the resolution of a case, prioritize a client-centered approach in your law firm. Ensuring a seamless and positive experience—for example, by simplifying the intake and payment process—increases the likelihood of clients sharing their satisfaction through reviews. Ask clients in a timely manner. Be proactive in asking your satisfied clients for reviews. Most will be happy to provide feedback on their experience with your firm, especially if it’s a positive one. Make the process easy for them by providing clear instructions and accessible platforms for leaving reviews. Address constructive feedback. Actively seek feedback from clients who have used your services. When they provide suggestions on areas for improvement, respond positively and strive for continuous improvement. Demonstrating responsiveness to feedback not only enhances your services but also reinforces your commitment to delivering the highest level of client satisfaction.
