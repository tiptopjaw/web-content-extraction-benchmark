Headphones Coloring Page Materials Step by Step Directions Time needed: 45 minutes Draw a Headphone Step by Step Draw a wide upside down curve. Make another smaller curve inside. Draw a smaller curve inside. Start the earpiece on the right. Draw a matching shape on the left. Add matching inside shapes. Draw cap shapes on the outside. Finish with even smaller cap shapes.
