BookMan Founder • Editor E-readers Kindle, Boox, PocketBook, Kobo, reMarkable Do you find yourself flying through books faster than your budget can keep up? Whether you’re a casual reader or a bookworm who devours multiple titles a week, e-book subscription services offer an affordable and convenient way to read more for less. Gone are the days when these platforms only featured indie authors—now they include bestsellers, audiobooks, magazines, and more. In 2025, the variety and flexibility of these services make them an excellent choice for anyone looking to expand their reading list. Here’s a breakdown of the top e-book subscription platforms to consider this year. 1. Kindle Unlimited Best for : Variety and global availability. Cost : $11.99/month (U.S.) Highlights : - Borrow up to 20 titles at a time. - Works across Kindle devices, phones, tablets, and computers. 2. Kobo Plus Best for : Flexible plans and Kobo device users. Cost : Multiple tiered plans (ebooks, audiobooks, or both). Highlights : - Available in select countries (e.g., U.S., UK, Canada, Australia). - 30-day free trial. 3. Everand (by Scribd) Best for : Bestsellers from major publishers. Cost : - Plus: $16.99/month (3 premium titles/month) - Highlights : - Credit-based model with access to bestselling titles. - Includes magazines, podcasts, sheet music, and Everand Originals. 4. Overdrive Libby Best for : Free access via public libraries. Cost : Free with a library card. Highlights : - App supports offline reading/listening and Kindle delivery. - Available on iOS, Android, and Kobo e-readers. 5. Epic! Best for : Kids under 12. Cost : Subscription required (free for teachers). Highlights : - Includes titles from major publishers and Epic Originals. 6. Storytel Best for : Audiobook lovers and families. Cost : Varies by region. Highlights : - Seamless switch between text and audio. - Multi-profile family support. 7. Blinkist Best for : Nonfiction summaries. Cost : Subscription required. Highlights : - Great for learning on the go. 8. 24symbols Best for : Multilingual reading. Cost : Low monthly fee. Highlights : - Works on virtually any device with a browser. These services cater to different needs—whether you want bestsellers, kids’ books, audiobooks, or nonfiction summaries—there’s a 2025 subscription option for you.
