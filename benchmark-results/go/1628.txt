How Long Does It Take to Get Certified as a Consultant? The time it takes to become a certified consultant depends on your chosen specialization, prior experience, and the certification program’s requirements. Generally, you can finish courses from one month to two years: Quick (1-4 weeks): Self-paced online courses like Google Project Management Certificate and HubSpot Sales or Marketing Consultant Certifications can be finished in under a month. Moderate (5-12 weeks): Many online certifications that provide additional learning resources could be finished within this timeframe (e.g., Project Management Professional (PMP) and Certified Business Analysis Professional (CBAP)) Extended (over 12 weeks): Certifications that require rigorous coursework and supervised training like Certified Management Consultant (CMC) and Six Sigma Black Belt take months to complete.
