Periodontal (gum) disease is the most common cause of tooth loss among adults. It affects about 46% of adults ages 30 or older ; about 9% of adults have severe periodontal disease. 1 Problems with your teeth, gums, or other parts of the mouth can increase your risk of having some diseases or health conditions. Small steps, like seeing a dentist regularly for checkups, can help you protect your mouth and teeth and live well at any age. The A Healthy Mouth for Every Body campaign offers a variety of materials to help you and your family take good care of your oral health.
