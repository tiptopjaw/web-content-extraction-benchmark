This post was updated thanks to help from Andrew Shell. I have a free account at Inoreader. I was reminded today that they support dynamic OPML subscription lists, and decided to give it a try. You can subscribe to an OPML subscription list. Exactly the same format we used for importing lists. This means I can use the same feed list in two readers. Or I could share my list with everyone who subscribes to my newsletter. I can update the list, and the flow of news to the subscribers changes too. I tried subscribing to my podcast list in Inoreader. It worked. I now can see my podcast updates in Inoreader, exactly as if it was in FeedLand. And when I add new feeds they show up over there, same when I remove. It'll be very interesting to see how it changes over time. I'll let you know! 😉 Links
