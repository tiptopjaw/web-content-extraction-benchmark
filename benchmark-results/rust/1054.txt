Its benefits include enhanced efficiency, scalability, and risk mitigation at every stage of the machine learning model life cycle. \n                         \n                     \n                         \n                                MLOps seeks to help enterprises meet the following goals: \n                             \n                         \n                             \n                                 Speeding up machine learning model experimentation and development  \n                                 Streamlining machine learning model deployment into production  \n                                 Continuously ensuring the advanced analytics solution quality and alignment with business needs  \n                             \n                         \n                     \n                 \n             \n                     \n                            Data privacy concerns the governance of sensitive data. It establishes how authorized parties can gain access to, modify​,​ and use it. It also determines their roles and level of access, as well as how the data is collected and distributed. The notion of data privacy is the subject of multiple regulations, such as the General Data Protection Regulation in the European Union. \n                         \n                     \n                            Data security, in turn, is the use of rules, practices​,​ and technology meant to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive data and its corruption. It can involve introducing rule-based access to data, data encryption​,​ and automated backups, among others. \n                         \n                     \n                            Data security doesn’t guarantee data privacy: the data processor can still mishandle the collected personal data while keeping it secure. Data privacy, however, requires the data processor to have the capability to protect the data against theft, corruption​,​ or unauthorized access. \n                         \n                 \n             \n                         \n                                The transformation into an insights-powered organization consists of: \n                             \n                         \n                             \n                                 Consolidation .
