Our office is funded from the Student Activity Fee (we get around $9 from each student), which was assessed along with tuition and other charges on your bursar bill. This means that our advice and representation are available at no extra charge. Usually, the only out-of-pocket expenses for our student clients are court fees.\n\nHow it works\n\nStart by telling us about your legal issue. Complete this online form  to schedule an appointment. From there, we will determine how we can best help you.\n\nWe employ four full-time attorneys who are licensed in the state of Indiana and more than two dozen second- and third-year law students from the IU Maurer School of Law. An intern, working under the supervision of one of our attorneys, will serve as your primary contact with the office. Our attorneys supervise every aspect of our interns’ work, and your intern will only take action on your case with your permission, and with the approval of their supervising attorney.\n\nConfidentiality\n\nOur attorneys and interns are bound by the Indiana Rules of Professional Conduct. Attorney-client confidentiality is an essential element of any attorney-client relationship, and our relationship with our student clients is no exception.\n\nMany of our student clients want their parents or other trusted adults in on their cases. We will ask for your express consent before we discuss your case with anyone else, even your parent or guardian."}
