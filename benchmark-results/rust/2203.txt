Improvements to cabinets and shelving are the most common upgrades, followed by updates to showers and tubs.\n\nKitchen renovation\n\nWhile the average kitchen remodel costs $26,934, homeowners typically spend $14,600 – $41,485. Labor and electrical costs will also heavily influence the final cost.\n\nHome exterior renovation\n\nExterior remodels can focus on the structural integrity or curb appeal of your home.
