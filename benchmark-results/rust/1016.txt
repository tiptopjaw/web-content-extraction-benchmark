Who funds it?\n\nYour participation in the training program will be covered by your Amazon Career Choice payment benefit, with any additional costs for the program being covered by Correlation One. If you enroll you will not need to pay for the program out of your own pocket.\n\nUnder certain country tax rules, tuition paid by Career Choice is viewed as taxable income. Amazon provides additional compensation to Career Choice participants to offset the income tax impact, but cannot guarantee it will cover all taxes. You may see a tax for this benefit applied to a future paycheck from Amazon. For further information, please consult Amazon Career Choice policy for your country.\n\nApplication process\n\nWho should apply?\n\nWe encourage any Amazon associates interested in learning and exploring new career options to apply. Please note to take part in the program you must be eligible for Career Choice benefits. In the US, full-time or part-time (20-29 hours per week) hourly L1-L4 blue badge employees that have been employed for 90 continuous days are eligible to participate in the program. In Canada, France, Germany, Spain, Italy and the United Kingdom, full-time or part-time (20-29 hours per week) hourly L1-L3 blue badge employees that have been employed for one year are eligible to participate in the program.\n\nThis includes all hourly non-exempt FC, CS, corporate, AWS, Amazon Fresh Stores, Amazon Books, Amazon 4-star, Amazon Pop Up, Amazon Go and certain retail and eligible subsidiary associates.\n\nAre there any prerequisites to apply?\n\nThere are no prerequisites to apply. Beginners are welcome, and you do not need to have any previous training or knowledge to succeed here.\n\nWhat is the application process?\n\nThe application process is an easy two step process consisting of a written application and a short skills assessment. Our process is not meant to be intimidating but a chance to get to know you better along the way! Our staff is here to guide you through the process each step of the way.\n\nWhat is on the Assessments?\n\nThe assessment will test your knowledge of verbal and mathematical reasoning, technology use, and basic data analytics problem-solving. Some of the questions may be difficult, which is normal. The point isn’t to get everything right but to show what you know and how you think so that we can curate the curriculum to your learning needs.\n\nCan I apply to multiple Career Choice programs with Correlation One now?\n\nYou should only apply to one program for now. You may select on your application if you want to be considered for additional programs for which you’re eligible.\n\nWhen should I apply?\n\nAs soon as possible. Training program seats are offered on a rolling basis.\n\nHow will I learn whether I got into the program?\n\nA member of our team will reach out to let you know whether you’ve been accepted into the program. They will also let you know how to make sure you’re ready for the program on day one!\n\nCourse content\n\nCan I keep doing my regular job during the program?\n\nOf course. Our goal is to build the most flexible program for people with busy work and personal lives. With virtual content and an instructor staff ready to accommodate your needs, you’ll be able to succeed regardless of how busy you are. Career Choice also provides Amazon Associates with the ability to apply for school accommodations to reduce or change your work schedule to meet your needs as you complete the course.\n\nWhat happens if I leave Amazon after starting the course?\n\nIf you leave Amazon for another job during the course you do not have to pay back tuition and you will be able to finish the course!\n\nWho teaches the course?\n\nThe course is taught by Correlation One training instructors. They’re great at making concepts easy to understand and practical, so that you can use them in the real world.\n\nWhere are the classes held?\n\nClasses are fully virtual, held in our “Live Online” classroom. This course will require you to have access to a computer (laptop or desktop), that can access the internet. There are no specifications required, but a tablet (iPad), Chromebook, or cellphone will NOT be sufficient to complete this training program.\n\nDo I need any special equipment to take the class?\n\nLearners will need a functioning laptop or desktop computer with an internet connection during class time. We will provide everything else you need.\n\nHow will I find potential new jobs through the program?\n\nOur program is designed to help you in your job search every step of the way. A dedicated Career Success Coach will work with you to understand your unique dreams and vision. From there, we’ll match you with roles that might be a fit. You’ll also be able to hear from top companies during live Career Fairs and events.\n\nI can't make the scheduled sessions, can I move classes to another day of the week?\n\nUnfortunately, we do not provide another schedule for our training programs. If you're concerned about a conflict with your work schedule, we will provide you with an admission/accommodation letter and welcome packet to request an accommodation from your manager.\n\nHave a question about the program or need help?\n\nNew Skills.
