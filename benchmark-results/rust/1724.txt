They can all be completed within an hour.\n\nFor each tutorial, we have multiple ways of accessing them:\n\nClick the tutorial link to open up a project directly in the App Inventor environment with the tutorial in the sidebar. Note that each of these can also be used for Hour of Code events.\n\nIf you prefer, you can click on the video links to follow the tutorial in a video.
