But I do have a general idea of how many games I read, how many games I played, and what I played most.\n\nYou might have number of sessions/hours played, number of people/groups played with, number of games purchased/backed, etc. Whatever data you kept and want to share, go for it!\n\nSuperlatives I’m Using\n\nAgain, you might not want to use the same “awards” as me, and that’s very fine and in fact I encourage it! I don’t know what my list will look like for sure, but some I am considering:\n\nMost Hacked Game\n\nGame Most Likely to Be on My Mind Still in 2026\n\nFavorite Playtested Game\n\nPDF Game I Need for My Shelf\n\nGame That Got My GM/Referee/MC Wheels Spinning\n\nIdeally this is a celebration of the games you played or read this year – I would caution against making purely negative Endie superlatives. Can’t wait to see what you played and read this year.\n\nCheers, LT"}
