How much does a kitchen remodel cost in 2025? A kitchen remodel varies widely based on square footage, material quality, and design changes. What’s the ROI of a bathroom remodel versus a kitchen remodel? Both kitchen and bathroom remodels deliver strong ROI. What are the typical permit requirements for a home remodel? Permit requirements vary by location, but any remodel involving structural changes, HVAC upgrades, or electrical rewiring will likely need municipal approval.
