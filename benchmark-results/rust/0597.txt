Beginning as a spiritual discipline passed down through generations, it has evolved into different practices and philosophies.\n\nThe word yoga comes from the Sanskrit root yuj, meaning “to unite” or join. This concept of unity lies at the heart of yoga, bringing together the body, mind, and spirit.\n\nModern Yoga\n\nAncient Practice,\nModern World\n\nYour yoga practice can take many forms. From movement, breathwork, singing, volunteering, studying ancient wisdom, meditating, and connecting to something bigger than yourself.\n\nYoga can also be more than a personal practice—it is a powerful tool for collective well-being. Specialized training is required to teach these classes.\n\nIn addition to the types of yoga identified above, it is common for a yoga teacher or yoga school to identify with a style, tradition, or lineage.\n\nEach approach has benefits, and there is no “right way” to practice yoga.
