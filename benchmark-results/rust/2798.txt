Each year, the IoT/Connected Product Awards spotlight the connected products that make lives better and that make integrators’ jobs easier.\n\nThe term “Internet of Things,” or IoT, refers to the network of physical devices, vehicles, wearables, home appliances and other tools embedded with electronics, software, sensors, actuators and connectivity. These capabilities enable connection and exchange of data. With features like auto-tracking, intelligent framing and image enhancements – compatible with hundreds of cameras across dozens of brands – Iris eliminates the need for additional hardware and reduces manual workloads, enabling teams to focus on creativity rather than configuration.\n\nIt’s an example of how technology can remove production challenges for large organizations, while also making high-quality production accessible to small teams, non-technical users and organizations with limited budgets.\n\nLearn more about Iris.\n\nAV-over-IP Device: Dante AVIO Install Adapters by Audinate\n\nThe Dante AVIO Install series of adapters are purpose-built to bring analog audio equipment into Dante networks for permanent installations.
