{"title":"Exploring USB Commands","author":"Gabriel Gonzalez","date":"2025-12-19T10:05:58+00:00","main_content":"If you’ve ever had to explore the interfaces of a USB-enabled device, you know how opaque and undocumented many of them can be. While working on this problem, I put together a small tool I now routinely use to discover commands and better understand how these devices behave.\n\nLike most tools born out of real needs, this one is very much a work in progress.
