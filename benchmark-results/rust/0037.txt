It compensates you up to the policy limits for medical expenses if you get sick or injured during your trip.\n\nSometimes called visitors insurance, these policies are designed for non-U.S. citizens traveling to the U.S. for trips lasting a few weeks or a few months, up to a year. For example, travel medical insurance is a common type of travel insurance for parents visiting the U.S. whose adult children live here.\n\nAmericans traveling abroad can also buy travel medical insurance, often as part of a comprehensive travel insurance policy that covers trip cancellation, travel delays and much more.\n\nTypes of Travel Medical Insurance for Visitors to the USA\n\nThere are two main types of travel medical insurance—also called travel health insurance — for visitors to the USA: fixed and comprehensive.\n\nFixed Medical Insurance\n\nFixed travel medical insurance is also called a fixed benefits policy or a limited policy. Fixed benefit plans are more affordable but have limited coverage.\n\nThese plans have a maximum fixed coverage amount for each medical service or condition. Deductibles apply for each event. For each injury or illness, you pay an initial deductible and the limited medical plan pays for the rest of the expenses covered by the policy.\n\nFixed medical insurance usually doesn’t include trip interruption coverage.\n\nComprehensive Medical Insurance\n\nComprehensive medical insurance policies are more expensive but provide more generous coverage than limited plans—for example, maximum coverage limits can be millions of dollars.\n\nThere are no sub-limits for coverage with comprehensive visitors insurance policies. You’re covered for medical costs up to the policy maximum, minus your deductible and coinsurance.\n\nTrip interruption, trip cancellation and travel delay benefits can be found in some comprehensive visitors insurance plans.\n\nWhat Does Travel Medical Insurance Cover in the USA?\n\nThe best travel medical insurance policies provide high levels of compensation for medical bills. Comprehensive travel medical insurance for visitors to the USA generally covers inpatient and outpatient services, urgent care and emergency medical services and emergency dental care.
