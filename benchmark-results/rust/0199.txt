Then keep reading for our top picks. \n\nWhat is graphic design? \n\nGraphic design is a creative discipline that involves combining art, technology, and design to create visual content to convey specific messages to targeted groups. The process involves the implementation of several different elements including color, visual hierarchy, page layout, typography, and imagery which are used to create harmonious visual compositions. While the primary goal of these visual compositions is to engage and inform an audience, a graphic designer also works closely with other design disciplines to ensure that their creations meet user needs and optimize the overall user experience. \n\nTraditionally graphic designers worked with print media collaterals such as poster design, brochures, magazines, and book publishing. However, these days there are more job opportunities in digital fields, such as website design and app creation. Fundamentally, whether online or in print, the aim of graphic design is to make information accessible, easy to understand, aesthetically pleasing, and meaningful –  irrespective of the particular niche the graphic designer is working in.\n\n8 top online graphic design schools \n\nNow that we’ve given you a short walkthrough of the field, let’s dive into the top eight online graphic design schools to consider in 2025. \n\n1.
