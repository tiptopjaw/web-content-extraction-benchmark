With teams now in both Riyadh and Dubai, we can better support clients on growth strategies, AI adoption, mergers & acquisitions, and strategic partnerships.”\n\nOther members of the senior team in the Middle East include Osama Al-Dosary (senior advisor), who previously served as President and CEO of telecom operator Salam, and as Deputy Minister for Communication Infrastructure in Saudi Arabia.
