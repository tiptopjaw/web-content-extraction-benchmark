Your video editing software will be your command center where you assemble your video projects and begin to see everything come together into one cohesive project. This rounds out our “Big Three” editing platforms that every video production professional should at least be familiar with.\n\nPros:\nAvailable for both Mac and PC\nCan handle nearly any audio or video format\nUsed widely among other professionals\nSeamlessly compatible with Pro Tools and other external creative software\n\nCons:\nMore complex than some other platforms\nDoesn’t work as seamlessly with Adobe platforms like After Effects\n\nRating on G2\n4.1/5 Stars\n\nPricing\nStarts at $39.99 per month for an individual license, with discounts available for paying yearly up-front.  \n\nGraphics and Special Effects Software\n\nIt’s easier than ever to add a whole slew of computer-generated effects to your projects, and as such, any video production professional should have at least a basic familiarity with programs that will allow you to add everything from motion graphic title cards to firearm muzzle flashes to your projects. \n\nAfter Effects\n\nAfter Effects is almost undisputedly the most-loved visual effects program out there.
