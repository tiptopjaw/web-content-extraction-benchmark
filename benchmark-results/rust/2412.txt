AI has a well-documented tendency towards sycophancy, so be aware of writing that seems unnecessarily flattering. That should be easy for someone as smart as you.\n\n✅ Grammar and style can also give the game away, for instance using emojis to help add an eye-catching visual element — and em dashes to give emphasis to a final clause.
