What car to rent, which company to choose, what insurance to take, where to go, where to eat, where to stop for the toilet. You know what I’m saying.\n\nYou are responsible for yourself. You have to follow the road conditions, weather forecasts, all Icelandic rules, etc. There’s no excuse for not knowing any of these.\n\nYou need to drive. Well. Driving in Iceland can be difficult if you are not used to driving in the countryside. And it’s especially difficult in winter when roads are icy or full of snow.\n\nHandling winter is challenging. The more so if you have to handle it alone without a local.\n\nGuided Tours Pros and Cons\n\nPros\n\nEverything is planned for you. The next stop, meal pauses, toilet pauses, parking, stop lengths, and more.\n\nA knowledgeable guide. The tour company handles literally everything, so you have to do virtually nothing. Including potentially tricky driving.\n\nCons\n\nYou pay for the guide and organization.
